Click for instructions on how you play the game
  1. You will be given a random number at the start of the game."Your number to match"
  2. There are four awesome veggies below.
    • Tomato
    • Carrot
    • Cucumber
    • Eggplant
  3. By clicking on a Veggie, you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.
  4. You win the game by matching your total score to the random number.
  5. You lose the game if your total score exceeds the random number value.
  6. The value of each Veggie is hidden from you until you click on it.
  7. Each time when the game starts, the game will change the values of each Veggie.
  8. And remember "Veggies rule!"
